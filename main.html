JOB PORTAL SYSTEM

A Job Portal website is an online platform that connects Job Seekers with Companies, offering a user-friendly interface for creating profiles, creating resumes, and specifying skills and preferences. Companies can post job listings and efficiently manage recruitment processes. These websites use advanced algorithms to match Job Seekers with relevant openings, simplifying job searches and enhancing recruitment for businesses. Key features include user registration, resume management, job posting, and secure data handling, making it a crucial bridge between Job Seekers and Companies, streamlining the job search process and improving recruitment efficiency.
